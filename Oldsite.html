Introduction

Here we strive to make the best popcorn to give your life a pop! Our popcorn is made from the finest ingredeints, we don't cheap out!

How we started

My son wanted to help those in need. After some research he decided that he would like to raise funds for World Vision because of the work they do with children.

Part of his fundraising efforts included selling flavoured popcorn to family, friends, and colleagues.

And so The POP! shop was born!

Our family embarked on creating the best flavoured popcorn. Our flavours diversified and we now make an assortment of flavoured popcorn from caramel and ranch.
​My son reached his fundraising goal of just over $1000 in 2016.
​
Every year we sell popcorn and all proceeds go to World Vision.
